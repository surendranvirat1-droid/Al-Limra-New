From Farm to Your Family

Al Limra was born from a simple belief: nature provides everything our bodies need. We carefully source the freshest fruits and vegetables, dehydrate them at optimal temperatures to lock in nutrients, and grind them into fine powders — free from any chemicals, additives, or preservatives.

Every batch is hygienically processed and quality-checked so you can trust what goes into your body and your family's meals.

  • Sourced from trusted, chemical-free farms
  • Dehydrated to preserve 95% of nutrients
  • Rich in vitamins, minerals & antioxidants
  • Easy to mix into any meal or drink
  • Long shelf life without preservatives
